About Street Conquest

It's basically an MMO where you're fighting to control the city.

Street Conquest: Map MMO RPG drops you into this massive online city where you've gotta make smart moves and team up with the right people if you want to run things.

You'll be growing your crew's power, taking on rival gangs, and jumping into massive city-wide battles as the game world changes around you.

It's all about smart planning mixed with quick multiplayer action - the kind of game that keeps you coming back for months.

We're going for that intense, movie-like vibe that gets your heart pumping.
